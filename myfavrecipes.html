Chicken Tikka

Prep: 30m Cook: 20m Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 800g boneless cubes
  • Cumin, Coriander & Garam Masala
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Mint chutney for serving

Instructions

  1. Refrigerate in marinade for 4+ hours.
  2. Thread onto skewers with small gaps.
  3. Grill at high temp until charred.
  4. Garnish with raw onion slices.

üí° Chef's Tip

Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 mins first so they don't catch fire on the grill!

Chole Bhature

Prep: 20m Cook: 45m Serves: 4

Highlights

  • Hand-kneaded Bhature dough
  • Tangy Amchur-infused Chole
  • Ginger-garlic aromatics

Quick Steps

  1. Rest Bhature dough for 30 mins.
  2. Simmer chickpeas in tomato-onion gravy.
  3. Deep fry bread until golden and puffed.
  4. Serve hot with lime pickles.
